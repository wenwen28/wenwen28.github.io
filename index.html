I got my Ph.D degree from the Institute of Automation, Chinese Academy of Sciences CASIA in 2017, and my advisor is Prof. Jason Geng. In 2014, I participated in the "Full body performance acquisition" project as a visiting student at Prof. Paul Debevec's Graphic Lab in University of Southern California (USC). In 2017, I finised the "Sparse Photometric 3D Face Reconstruction" project under guidance of Prof. Jingyi Yu in Shanghai Tech University. From 2018, I jointed Tencent Youtu Lab as a senior researcher.

My research interest focus on Computational Photograph (light field capture and display), Computer Vsision (3D face reconstruction, human pose estimation).
